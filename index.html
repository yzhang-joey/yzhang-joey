    Biography

    Yi Zhang received the B.S. degree in software engineering from Xiamen University (XMU) in 2014. He received the M.S. and the Ph.D. degree in communication Engineering from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2016 and 2020, respectively. He was an assistant engineer at Quanzhou Institute of Equipment Manufacturing of Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) during 2016-2017. His primary research areas include wireless communication, game theory and optimization theory.
