  • Biography

  •  Yi Zhang received his B.S. degree in software engineering from Software College, Xiamen University (XMU), China, in 2014. He received his M.S. degree from Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering (GICE), National Taiwan University (NTU), Taipei, Taiwan, in 2016. He has been an assistant engineer in Quanzhou Institute of Equipment Manufacturing, Haixi Institutes, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), during 2016-2017. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in GICE at NTU. His primary research areas include wireless communication, game theory and optimization theory.
